# Privacy Policy

Launch0 is built with privacy as a core principle.

## Data Collection

**Launch0 does not collect, store, or share any personal data.**

- No analytics
- No crash reporting
- No user tracking
- No advertising SDKs
- No data leaves your device
- **Works fully offline** — Launch0 doesn't even request internet permission

## Permissions

Launch0 requests only the permissions it needs, and most are optional:

| Permission | Purpose | Required |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Home launcher | To function as your default launcher | Yes |
| Accessibility | Double tap to lock screen only | Optional |
| Usage stats | Show screen time on home screen | Optional |
| Microphone | Record voice notes in Notes only | Optional |
| Notification access | Hold notifications for Do Not Disturb only | Optional |

Launch0 does **not** request internet permission — it works completely offline.

## Accessibility Service

The accessibility service is used **exclusively** to enable the double-tap-to-lock-screen gesture. It is:

- Disabled by default
- Optional — the app works without it
- Does not collect or transmit any data

## Notes

Everything you add to Notes — text, to-dos, voice memos, and images — is stored **only on your device**. Voice notes require the microphone permission, which is used exclusively to record audio you choose to save. Nothing is uploaded or shared.

## Do Not Disturb

When you enable Do Not Disturb, Launch0 uses Android's notification access to hold notifications from the apps you select and release them later. Notification contents stay on your device and are never sent anywhere. This permission is optional and only needed if you use the feature.

## Wallpapers

Launch0 can generate a new wallpaper every hour. Wallpapers are created **on your device** using simple gradients and patterns — nothing is downloaded, and no data is sent.

## Open Source

Launch0 is fully open source. You can review the entire codebase at [github.com/ketansp/Olauncher](https://github.com/ketansp/Olauncher).

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, [open an issue](https://github.com/ketansp/Olauncher/issues) on GitHub.
